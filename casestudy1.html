Dataset Description

This Dataset talks about whether individuals can lend loan toother individuals based on various factors. The entire dataset is a list of people whose loans are granted. This dataset help easily identify the risk factor and whether or not we have lend loan to people whom we do not know based on certain factors like income, reason for loa, joint or individual application, what the home ownership status etc. We are aiming to calculate the interest rate that can be given to a person if loan is granted based on the status of the person.


I feel that the data is biased as the list of loans consist mostly of people paying off their loan taken and also all the individuals are people who have their loans granted. Hence, an unbiased dataset will help us predict a much better model whether we can give loan and the risk factor involved. The dataset is not clean and has lots of missing data. There are lots of categorical unwanted variables that may ruin the calculation of interest rate when training a model.

PreProcessing

I first looked at the data and decided the most important columns that can influence the decision of the Interest Rate. Columns like employee title were not of use and there were around 4000 titles and hence we can remove them. After choosing the right columns, I checked for null values in the columns and replaced them with zeros. I then took the categorical data and did one hot encoding and put them in a new data frame that was used for the model estimation.
